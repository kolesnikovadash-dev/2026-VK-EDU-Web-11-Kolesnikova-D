How to build a moon park?

I want to create an amusement park on the Moon. What technologies, materials, and energy sources would be required to make it possible?

asked 2 hours ago

Tags: space engineering
5

First of all, you need stable energy, life support systems, and safe transportation. Without solving these problems, building an entertainment park on the Moon would be impossible.

3

You should also think about construction materials, radiation protection, and gravity compensation. The project is not only about attractions, but also about survival conditions.
